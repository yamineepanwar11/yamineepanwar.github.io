PATTERN

Wall patterns can be used to add character and dynamism to a room.

First Thing

Homeowners and office managers alike are embracing stripes, dots, and more intricate designs, recognizing their power to inject dynamism and character into any area.Smooth finish: A clean, sleek look that's ideal.

Second Thing

Go big by painting an entire wall in your kid's room, the kitchen, or a hallway with chalkboard or whiteboard paint. Or keep things small and sweet by just framing.Smooth finish: A clean, sleek look.

Third Thing

Hang them using pant hangers: If you don't want to invest in pattern hooks and a hole punch, use regular pant hangers. You can keep smaller pieces in a bag, sheet protector, or envelope and clip that on the hanger.
